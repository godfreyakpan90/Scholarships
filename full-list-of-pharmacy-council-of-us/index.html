Full List of Pharmacy Council of US

Every licenced Pharmacist belongs to one or more Association. It is important to highlight some of the outstanding Associations of Pharmacists near you. It is worthy to note that a life well defend is life well lived. So this Association is tasked to advocate the role of Pharmacist to the people around them. This is only possible when this individual is licenced as professionals. In order to work as licence professionals, there is need to work as a team. Pharmacist Council of the United State continue to provide safe and secure space for pharmacist to exercise their professionalism in the US.
